Affine & Viewing Transformations

Part 1: Wireframe Cube (Isometric)

  • View Matrix: V = lookAt(eye, at, up)
  • Projection Matrix: P = orthographic(...)
  • Model Matrix: M = identity
  • Composite Transformation Matrix (CTM): CTM = P * V * M

Part 2: Cubes in Perspective Views

  • Cube 1 (1-point perspective): CTM = P * V * M where P = perspective(...), V = lookAt(...), M = identity
  • Cube 2 (2-point perspective): CTM = P * V * M where P = perspective(...), V = translate(...), M = identity
  • Cube 3 (3-point perspective): CTM = P * V * M where P = perspective(...), V = rotateX(...) * rotateY(...) * translate(...), M = identity
