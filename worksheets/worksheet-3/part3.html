Affine & Viewing Transformations

Part 1: Single Cube

  • View Matrix: V = lookAt(eye, at, up)
  • Projection Matrix: P = perspective(fovy, aspect, near, far)
  • Model Matrix: M = translate(tx, ty, tz)
  • Composite Matrix: CTM = P * V * M

Part 2: Three Cubes

  • Cube 1: M = translate(0.0, 0.0, 0.0), CTM = P * V * M
  • Cube 2: M = translate(1.5, 0.0, 0.0), CTM = P * V * M
  • Cube 3: M = translate(-1.5, 0.0, 0.0) * rotateX(angle), CTM = P * V * M

Notes

  • M = model matrix, positions/rotates the cube
  • V = view matrix, sets camera position and direction
  • P = projection matrix, applies perspective
  • CTM = P * V * M transforms vertices from model space to clip space
